About this Platform

The Roselle economic development platform is an all-in-one platform providing a centralized service to easily set up, and file for economic incentive programs.

Who Is This Best For?

This application is for new or existing businesses, consultants, banks, and others involved in and seeking the economic development of Roselle, IL. who want to learn about, quickly apply for, and manage incentives.
