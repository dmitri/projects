Evanston Business Economic Development Platform

Evanston currently has 4 active programs:


TIF Incentive Program

The City of Evanston has established the use of tax increment financing (TIF) as a strategic tool in its economic development program to foster redevelopment in areas of the community where needed.

Learn More & Apply
AARPA Grant

Program designed to utilize a portion of ARPA funds to assist businesses with the financial impacts of the pandemic, as well as support economic resiliency moving forward.

Learn More & Apply
Entrepreneurship Support Grant

The program is intended to assist in the growth of jobs in Evanston through entrepreneurship, helping residents and local business owners to create jobs by starting and retaining sustainable small businesses.

Learn More & Apply
Storefront Modernization

Storefront Modernization Program is a funding tool to assist small business owners improve the appearance and functionality of commercial buildings located in the city's business districts.
